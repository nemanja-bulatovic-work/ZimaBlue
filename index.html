our motive and goals

motive

We believe that in this copy/paste world, being truly creative is the greatest challenge of them all. Finding unique perspectives and understanding the true core of any project is the driving force of our creative process. Creativity is more than a simple tool for us - it's what makes our mind young, keeps our blood running and shapes our view of the world.

goals

We train our minds to find the hidden gems in any project. We aim to give clients a novel vision of their product, while involving them in the creative process in order to maximize productivity and satisfaction. We believe that the trends in the creative industries should move away from mass production that overwhelms the market, and towards individual solutions for each client and every product. Our agency is young, but we are not inexperienced. We are not green, but blue - Zima Blue. We might overwhelm you with questions, but our solutions will leave you overwhelmed with joy and satisfaction.
